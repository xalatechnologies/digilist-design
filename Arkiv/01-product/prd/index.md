## Requirements Traceability

All PRD requirements are tracked in the [requirements.json](../../requirements/requirements.json) file with unique IDs:

- **DOM-001** through **DOM-010**: Domain requirements
- **SAAS-001** through **SAAS-010**: Platform/SaaS requirements
- **TND-001** through **TND-009**: Tender requirements
- **SEC-001** through **SEC-004**: Security requirements

## Document Governance

### Review Process

- **Quarterly Reviews:** Main PRD reviewed quarterly by Product Team
- **Feature PRDs:** Reviewed when features are updated or new features added
- **Stakeholder Approval:** Product Owner, Engineering Lead, Design Lead

### Change Management

- **Version Control:** All changes tracked in Git
- **Change Log:** Document history maintained in each PRD
- **Approval:** Changes require Product Owner approval

### Maintenance

- **Owners:** Product Team
- **Contributors:** Product, Engineering, Design, Stakeholders
- **Update Frequency:** As needed for new features, quarterly for review
